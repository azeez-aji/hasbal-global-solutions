Client was manually reconciling HR/payroll data between 2024 and 2025 employee datasets — slow, error-prone, and unable to detect ghost employees, underage employment, or suspicious salary changes. Built a one-click automated audit framework in Excel using Power Query, replacing the entire manual process with 15 systematic checks across data integrity, salary anomalies, workforce movement, and compliance — reducing processing time by 60%. Delivered full Power Query training so the client now runs and extends the system independently.

🔴Data Integrity (7 checks)

DOB mismatch across years, missing DOB (2024/2025), DOB after employment date, employment after valuation date, DOB after valuation date.

🟡Salary Anomalies (2 checks)

Salary decrease detection, salary increase >50% flag.

🟢Workforce Movement (3 checks)

Missing from 2025 records (ghost employee detection), new hires 2025, new exits 2025.

🔵Compliance & Policy (3 checks)

Underage at employment, underage at valuation, over-60 at valuation (retirement policy).

▸Built 15 automated M Language audit queries across 3 source datasets (EmployeeData_2024, EmployeeData_2025, ExitData_2025)
