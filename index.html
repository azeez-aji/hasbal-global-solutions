The entire data pipeline was automated: an Excel workbook on OneDrive is continuously refreshed by a Power Automate flow I configured, feeding live data directly into Power BI with zero manual intervention.

📊 Executive Overview

Top-level KPIs (Total Projects, Completion Rate, Overall Score), project count by region (donut chart), monthly trend bar chart, and status breakdown — all filterable by date range, PM, and region.

Granular project-level table with Region, Month, Status, Avg Rating & Turnaround Days. Bar chart breakdown by PM showing workload distribution across all 6 managers.

Heatmap-style rating matrix scoring 10 criteria per project (Communication, Domain Knowledge, Framework, Infrastructure, etc.). Avg ratings by PM and by Region with conditional colour coding.

Turnaround Days trend line by project, Overall Experience by BPSS Month stacked bar, and a scatter plot of Turnaround Days vs Avg Rating segmented by all 5 regions.
